Salad with grilled peaches and blue cheese

To be honest, in the summer, different vegetable salads from the “must make and eat once a day” category turn into “how tired these salads are.” Therefore, closer to autumn, you are looking for some more interesting options, but still light and bright, summer is after all.

That's why it's great to experiment with seasonal vegetables, fruits, and herbs.

Today's salad may seem too bold, however, don't judge without trying. Cut the peaches and mango into pieces of any shape. I made large peach slices and a small mango cube.

Melt the honey and a little olive oil in a frying pan, add the peaches and fry, turning once, for 5 minutes on each side. It should form such a caramelized crust.

When the peaches are ready, add the mango to the same. Since the fruit is very tender, the pan can be safely removed from the heat, the mango will have enough residual heat. Stir well so that it is also soaked in caramel sauce.

Now flavouring. In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, water, and lemon zest.

Put the saucepan on the stove and cook until everything dissolves and starts to boil. At this point, add the lemon juice and simmer until the mixture thickens slightly, about two minutes.

Combine greens, peaches and mango in a bowl, add crumbled blue cheese and pour dressing over top. It turns out very interesting. If you love the taste of caramel and burnt sugar, you will understand me. Sweet peaches, bitter caramel crust and dressing, and salty blue cheese. Here is a combination for the brave in spirit!

Enjoy!
