Light Cold Gazpacho Style Soup

Gazpacho is one of the most popular Spanish dishes. However, this soup will fit perfectly in our hot Far Eastern summer. Just imagine - it's hot, you don't feel like doing anything, and here a refreshing lunch comes to the rescue. Cold, with bright tomato accents and spicy aromas of pepper and spicy tabasco. Already the first spoon will give you a cool freshness, bordering on the invigorating spiciness of the ingredients. Ready!

Cut 5 tomatoes and 1 pepper into quarters and remove seeds. With cucumbers (1-2 pcs.), Do the same, just peel it off.

Chop all the vegetables and onions into large cubes, put in a deep cup.

Add extra virgin olive oil (150 gr.), If you have it with spicy additives, it will be even better! A little milk (30 gr.), wine vinegar (1 tablespoon) and tabasco (you can replace chili).

Mix well and refrigerate for an hour and a half. During this time, the vegetables are well marinated and saturated with aromas.

Before serving, transfer everything to a blender bowl and puree for 3 minutes. If it seems to you that the soup is too thick, dilute it with mineral water and puree again for another minute. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Ready soup will go well with garlic sauce. To do this, squeeze a clove of garlic into 100 grams of cream (33%), stir and pour over the gazpacho. A couple of crackers - and here it is, your personal cool paradise in the middle of a hot metropolis.

Enjoy!
