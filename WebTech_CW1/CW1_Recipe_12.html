Christmas English Cake

English New Year's cupcake needs to be prepared today! Historically, the British have made special cupcakes for weddings or Christmas holidays. It was a real, valuable, important gift. The cupcake I'm talking about is called a fruit cake. For all its simplicity, the dessert is fraught with incredible, even fantastic properties. More than half of the total mass is occupied by candied fruits. This is necessarily dried raisins, a lot of dried berries and, of course, candied citrus fruits. All this is abundantly soaked in rum with various spices. Then they are heated in order to evaporate excess moisture. At this time, spices in hot alcohol are revealed even stronger and brighter. Such a mass is added to the dough and baked in the oven for a long time in order to decently dry the future cake.

The finished cake is wrapped in parchment and stored in a dry place at room temperature. Once a week it is unwrapped and soaked in rum. Again and again, week after week. Usually the impregnation process takes about one to one and a half months. After that, the cake remains in storage. Every day, candied fruits, rum, spices, dough are saturated with aromas and tastes of each other, the dough becomes a pleasant, dense texture and becomes one with candied fruits. The British covered such soaked cupcakes with marzipan. What provided long-term storage - from a YEAR and LONGER! That's why they started preparing it half a year before an important event, it, like wine, only got better.

First of all, let's start with candied fruits. It takes time to properly prepare them, so you can do it in an hour and a half (even at night). We have collected all the best so that the taste of the finished cupcake will not disappoint you.

Choose the taste and type of alcohol yourself. It can be rum, cognac. Take something of high quality so that the taste is pleasant, soft and correct.

Collect all the candied fruits in a large bowl: white raisins (200 g), black raisins (200 g), dried cranberries (150 g), dried cherries (150 g), candied lemon (100 g), candied orange (200 g). We used wet lemon strips and cut them into cubes, so it came out juicier. The same was with the kumquat (it is large). Please note that candied citrus fruits must be juicy.

Prepare 200-250 grams of dark rum. We found spiced rum. But in general, you can take the one that you like more.

In a mortar (or blender), crush cardamom (4-7 pcs), cloves (8-12 pcs), star anise (3-4 pcs).

Pour rum into candied fruits, add ground spices (sift through a sieve).

Stir and heat in a pan. It is necessary that the rum did not boil, but simply evaporated. Stir like this for 5-10 minutes. First, we dry the liquid; secondly, we reveal the aroma of spices in hot rum. At the same time soften the candied fruit.

Lay them out on a baking sheet to cool down quickly.

It is important that the ingredients are at room temperature, take them out in advance. This year we are preparing the most basic but fantastically delicious cupcake. We will be pleased if you leave feedback at the end of the year, what you liked, how it turned out. So that Anya and I can make a new recipe even better and tastier!

Use quality butter (200 g), this is very important. We used New Zealand.

Beat butter and sugar panel (200 g) with a mixer until fluffy.

Next, add eggs (200 g) in portions. Then rum (30 g). Beat well again for 5-10 minutes.

At this time, we make blanks from siliconized parchment.

Make a round bottom with a diameter of 18 cm and make a wide strip of parchment, cut on one side by 5 cm.

Lay parchment in a 18 cm metal ring (as in the photo), then insert the bottom.

Add flour (250g) and almond flour (65g). Here we add a cinnamon (7 g).

Next almonds (100 g). We dried it in the oven (150 degrees, 8 minutes). Chop it up a bit and add it to the batter.

Then all the candied fruits. Mix well. Get a viscous mass.

Put the dough into the ring. We bake at 150-160 degrees for about 1.5-2 hours. Unlike regular cupcakes, this one takes a long time to bake. We need to dry the candied fruits and the dough itself even more. Expect a good blush. And check with a skewer, it should be dry. The more we dry the cake, the more he will then take rum.

Get it like this. Lubricate it with rum (30 g).

Now wrap the cake in parchment and film. Once a week we will open it and smear it with rum again (20-30 grams). And so you need to do a month or even longer. And on New Year's Eve open and serve.

Then decorate and serve or give away!

Enjoy!
